About Me

I’m currently a software engineer at Waymo, working with the simulation team to develop a self-driving car you can trust! I previously developed simulation tools for autonomous vehicles at Applied Intuition. Prior to that, I worked on maps at Argo AI.

Regarding my academic background, I was a post-doctoral fellow in Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University working with Professor Peter Steenkiste. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Southern California, where I was a member of the Autonomous Networks Research Group (ANRG). My advisor was Professor Bhaskar Krishnamachari.

I earned my Bachelor of Engineering and Master of Engineering degrees in Electrical Engineering from The Cooper Union in New York, NY.
